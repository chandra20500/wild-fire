What is Wild Fire ?

Wildfire is India's oldest and the most prestigious rock band competition which turned international last year. With prizes worth lakhs of rupees and your prestige at stake, Wildfire has been the launch pad for many contemporary bands including -Underground Authority, Parikrama, Kryptos, Zygnema, Cassini's Division and many more.


Be ready to fulfill your love for rock at the Wildfire Finals at Kharagpur! Wildfire is a western rock band competition, open to any college or semi-professional band from India and abroad.

PRIZE
  • Cash prize INR 1 Lakh
  • Chance to open for the headlining band at Spring Fest, IIT Kharagpur in front of 30,000+ crowd.
  • Winner band will feature on an episode of Maed in India. This will include,
    Recording the interview and music performed on the show at a reputed music studio in Mumbai
    Mixing and mastering the music performed on the show
    Distributing the audio on all major podcast apps like Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts, JioSaavn, TuneIn, Stitcher, etc.
    Two minute-long video promos along with a set of behind the scenes photos from the episode.
    The total cost for producing the episode is about Rs. 2.5 lacks.
  • Winner will get an 8-hour free recording session at Resonance Studios and Academy worth Rs.1 lack.
  • Winners will get free training at music camp at Swarnabhoomi Music academy worth INR 1.25 lakhs and runner up will get 50% off on this camp.
  • Songdew will release the music of the winning band/artist globally to over 150 digital platforms including Spotify, Saavn, Amazon Music,
    Apple Music, Gaana, Google Play Music, etc. Promote the release on Songdew TV (which has a reach of around 40 Million Households).
RULES
  • Offline prelims will be conducted in Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata and Guwahati. Online prelims will also be conducted wherein participants must send a link to their video performance at wildfire.sf2020@gmail.com.
  • After the first round, a shortlist of bands would be released who will qualify to perform at the main event at IIT Kharagpur.
  • The main event at IIT Kharagpur would consist of 2 rounds: prefinals and finals. 4. In prefinals at IIT Kharagpur, a group will be required to perform at least three numbers of their choice within 20 minutes. (Amps on to amps off and inclusive of sound check)
  • In finals, a group will be required to perform at least 4 numbers of their choice within 40 minutes (Amps on to amps off inclusive of sound check).
  • Bands will be disqualified on the spot for misconduct, obscenity or foul language, and will be banned from performing at all subsequent editions of spring fest.
  • Only English and Hindi Rock Music are allowed
  • Equipment provided will be:
    a. One bass amplifier speaker
    b. One lead amplifier speaker
    c. Adequate microphones d. Drum set (Participants can bring along with your own cymbals and chokes/high hats)
  • Bands will have to bring their own musical instruments and special effects. However, programmed music is not allowed.
  • Spring Fest will not be held responsible for the failure of any instrument.
